Talk to Animals — Understand Every Species

FaunaTalk is a universal animal translator: real‑time audio translation, species‑aware context, non‑invasive listening, and conservation tools — designed for pet owners, field researchers, and wildlife lovers.

How FaunaTalk Works

Real-time Translation

High‑fidelity audio capture converts animal vocalizations into human language in real time, and vice versa — with adjustable tone and context.

Species‑Aware Models

Trained models for mammals, birds, marine life, reptiles, and insects — each model understands species‑specific signals and behavior cues.

Non‑Invasive Listening

Directional microphones and ethical sampling protect animal welfare — no tagging or invasive procedures required for most use cases.

Conservation Mode

Aggregate anonymous signals for researchers to monitor population health, stress indicators, and migration with privacy protections.
